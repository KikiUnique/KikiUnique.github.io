A new passage way to the Border Lands has opened in the north.

The children of stories have completed a ritual causing duplicates of people to start wandering the local area.

Appearances of Goblins with an obsession for books and writing has recently been on a rise.

Root and Branch and its followers are currently looking for an individual with red hair and eyes carrying a faded green pre-arrival backpack. Root and Branch themselves are offering a boon for their capture.

The cult of the dead god are looking for something. They are being secretive about what they are looking for. Rumour is that a large charge stone has gone missing, they seek its return in exchange for a future favour from the cult.
