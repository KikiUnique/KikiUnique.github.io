The Neverwas is a world made of the lost and forgotten places of our world. The places that make the Neverwas are the still living places from the world, leaving behind a a dead place. The geography is not consistent and changes over time and not all places can e reached soley by travelling through the Neverwas. The Neverwas is full of cracks and gaps that bleeds between the Neverwas and the world. These gaps shift and grow over time, existing ones shifting or closing, and new ones opening. As the world changes and areas gain qualities qualities to places of the Neverwas the gaps may shift or new ones form.
