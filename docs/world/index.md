The world was once a place of magic when the Fey Otherworld had its connections with World. But during the medieval periods the Fey left the World. Very little accurate information remains regarding the Fey and their departure. Some documents say they were defeated, the magic faded so they couldn't survive, or that the spread of iron made the world to hazardous for them and they close the paths between worlds. Regardless of the truth the fey left leaving behind the humans.

Humans had the world to themselves for centuries, many generations growing, learning, and building, bending the world to their will. Then some time in the 21st century the planets environment reached a tipping point and the fey returned. With a burst magic returned to the world in a flood from the Otherwrold. Old woods sprouted, grew, and aged in a matter of minutes as if they had been there for centuries. Plant growth ran riot overgrowing much of the world. In less than a day the world became a wild place, many of our roads covered with asphalt cracked and tore up, our buildings cracked and broken. And with the plant growth came the Fey and the creatures of the Otherworld, walking the long sealed paths to our World.

The next few years was chaotic. We found our technology ceased working, electricity would not flow in our devices, our guns would not fire, the gunpowder having turned to ash in the bullets, and the fuel in our engines had turned to water. The fey began the great wild hunt. Years of being away left they hungry for the hunt and their favoured prey were humans. With the hunt stalking humans, dangerous creatures for the Otherworld roaming the World, and the technology we relied upon no longer functioning we were not prepared to defend ourselves. Some survived by hiding, some by fighting and proving themselves, and some make deals with those Fey either friendly toward or needing something from the Humans.

By the end of the great wild hunt most of the human population were either dead or taken by the fey in exchange for sparing their life. Those left in the World found most of the Fey had returned to the Otherworld but many still remained in the World for their own reasons. After the Great Wild Hunt the emergence of Feylings, humans with a Fey Lineage began to appear. Some are old bloodlines from before the Fey left the world with the lineage manifesting now the magic has returned, other the descendants of those taken during the Great WIld Hunt and held in a domain of the OtherWorld where time passes more quickly. Despite their heritage the Feylings are not claimed by any of the Fey and are considered nothing but a human with a handful of tricks.

It has been years since then and the world has reached a new status quo. The humans ae living in the remnants of the World. When ever a settlement gets too large or draws too much attention the Fey stage a Hunt in the area. Each year on the anniversary of the Great Wild Hunt a smaller hunt takes place across the world. Now the humans and the Feylings are just trying to survive in their new world of magic, dangerous creatures, and Fey with the power and strength to do what they please.
