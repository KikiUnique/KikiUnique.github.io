## The Otherworld Overview ##

The Otherworld is the world of magic and the Fey. The normal rules of our world do not hold true there and can change as you travel. Time can speed up or slow down making your hour-long journey there have you return mere seconds or 100 years after you left. Gravity can fluctuate, things can react differently such as water than burns cold, or stone as dense as gas. The wise takes nothing for certain even in commonly travels paths.
The Otherworld is a place of magic and influence with the fey with the influence wielding it as instinctively as humans breathe. The Otherworld is controlled by the High Fey that have influence over their domain and between these domains are the wilds and the paths through the wilds. These domains appear based on the nature of the High Fey that rules it with the High Fey being a force of nature within their domain and absurdly powerful out of it.
The Wilds are the wilds places between domains, not rules over by any High Fey and a source of raw magic. The unaligned fey live in the wilds, with no High Fey to answer to they are free to act in any way they wish. The Wilds also contains the Wild Paths, the free pathways that wind through the Wilds allowing travel without entering another High Feys domains. The raw magic and nature of the wilds makes they unsafe for those that would travel they without caution. Creatures and Fey of different types live in the Wilds and not all of them take well to others using the Wilds Paths.
The Wilds and the Wild Paths fluctuate but the well-prepared that can learn of a current path can travel 100s of miles with just a short journey if they can protect themselves.
