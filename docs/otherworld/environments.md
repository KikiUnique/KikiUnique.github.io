# The Otherworlds Environments #
The Otherworld is full of unusual and in some cases very bizarre places to explore. When entering the Otherworld from the World the traveller finds themselves in the Wild Paths. From there if they leave the Paths they enter the Wilds and if they continue to travel deeper into the Wilds they can find themselves entering the High Fey Domains.

## The High Fey Domains
The High Fey domains are each unique with unique environments and in some cases unique creatures. Before the Fey return there were only a couple courts well known to world, mixed up in myth and story and called, by some, the summer and winter courts. After the Fey left the world the domains were untethered from time. As the centuries passed for the World, the millennia passed for the Otherworld. High Fey rose and well, the old courts were no more, new High Fey, in greater numbers, flourished and formed domains of their own. Whilst there are many High Fey only a small number are currently active in the local area.

### The Court of Twilight 
![Capital City of the court of twilight](/assets/img/otherworld/environment/twi2.jpeg){: .align-right}
The Court of Twilight is ruled over by a High Fey that exists on the borders of things, transitions, and liminal spaces. The entrances to the Paths closest to their domain can be found in empty hallways, stairwells, and old transit hubs. Not all the paths are permanent, passing at twilight.
The Fey that share this domain are interested in things that pass, change, and altering the state of things. The domain of the Court of Twilight is in perpetual twilight, a city of alleys and hallways, hubs, and doorways. You will find no rooms here, just transient spaces.

### The Midnight Conclave
The Midnight Conclave is ruled over by a High Fey that is the embodiment of the void. They personify absolute darkness, absence, and separation. The entrances to the Paths closest to their domain can be found in places where the sunlight does not reach or where a great loss has happened and the darkness time of a clouded night is upon you. 
The Fey that share this domain are interested in concealment, obscuring facts, and breaking things apart to their basics elements. The domain of the Midnight Conclave is shrouded by a perfect darkness, lights brought to the domain barely shine more than a few feet. The lands are sparse with huge bottomless gulfs between the lands. 

### The Teller of Lies
The Teller of Lies is ruled over by a High Fey that hungers for stories, information, and knowledge. Despite this the Teller of Lies does not share the knowledge readily. They personify hidden secrets, weaving fo stories, and lies. The entrances closest to Paths closest to their domain can be found in places of study, centers of stories, and places where lies are oft told.
The Fey that share this domain are interested in gathering knowledge, enacting stories, keeping secrets, and trading information. The domain of the Teller of Lies is a sprawling, brightly lit, library full of secret passages, false shelves, and hidden rooms. Every book contains knowledge gained by the Teller of Lies and their underlings. The deeper into the domain, the more secretive the information becomes and the fewer Fey allowed to access it.

### The Bone Guard
The Bone Guard is ruled over by a High Fey that has become tied to corpses, death, and the transition from life to death. The entrances to the Paths closest to their domain can be found in graveyards, places of great disaster, and may occasionally often upon the death of a living creature.
The Fey that share this domain are collectors of tooth, claw, bone, and on occasion, corpses. It is not clear what these Fey do with the items them gather but those tied to the Bone Guard are often found wearing bones, teeth, or claws. The domain of the Bone Guard resembles a large sprawling castle constructed of a smooth white stone in the often regions. The closer to the center of the domain the stone walls give way to walls of bone. 

### The Lost
The Lost is ruled over by a High Fey that none have ever seen, the embody absence, disconnection, and lost things. The entrance of the Paths closest to their domain are hidden in places not easily found and once they are found and used they close and reopen elsewhere. 
The Fey that share this domain are finders of lost things and hiders of found things. It is said that once a Lost Fey hides something from you it will never be found by you or those you are connected with. The domain of the Lost is a large woodland with small wooden settlements. The woods are ever shifting, moving the trees and settles throughout the say. Unless you have a way to find a path through the domain the constant change in environment makes this domain very difficult to navigate.

### The Burning Eclipse
The Burning Eclipse is ruled over by a High Fey that personifies environmental change, extremes, and suppression of things. The entrances to the Paths closest to their domain are not permanent, they only appear in places and times of extremes. When extreme storms form, when a tsunami hits, when the environment is extreme a Path opens. It is believed that during an eclipse or when a settlement becomes ruled by an extreme leader and the people are suppressed Paths to the Burning Eclipse can be found in the settlement.
The fey that share this domain are hardened to extreme environments and tend to be tied an emotion felt at its extreme with difficultly experiencing others. These fey struggle when outside the environment they are hardened to and with the extreme differences in emotion making interaction difficult most tend towards isolation unless they find a place or person that matches their mindset. The domain of the Burning Eclipse is lit in low light from a permanent eclipse with a ring of burning flame just visible around the edge. The lands of the domain are divided as different sections are experiencing extremes with no transition between them, blizzards besides burning heat waves besides huge storms besides a tornado beside floods, and on. The domain is filled with extreme weathers, disasters, and natural occurrences in extreme examples of terrain.

## The Wilds
The Wilds are a place of chaos that appears as a great and overgrown forest where life grows riotously and creatures of all sorts from the World and the Otherworld roam there. The creatures of the World came there long ago and have since grown larger, more dangerous, and instinctively know those that belong in the Otherworld and those that do not, they will defend their home violently should intruders get too close.
Beyond the wildlife there are the Wild Fey. These fey owe not allegiance to the High Fey and their domains, instead living in the wilds free from any influence other than their own. Travellers of the path may encounter the Wild Fey on their journey. The Wild Fey are highly chaotic in their thoughts and opinions and each group you encounter are highly variable in opinions of those from the World.
The Wild Fey tend to leave the World alone wanting to live in the Wilds in peaces, only entering the World or the domains when they must or need something. The most common visitors to either are the Goblins. These human looking creatures are creatures of obsession, they go where their obsession demand and leave once it is satisfied. Oddly, unlike most fey that need to use the entrances of the Wild Paths, the Goblins appear to manifest instantaneous entrances to the Paths when their Obsession grows or is satisfied. They seem to appear and disappear at will, although it is not really under their control.
The Wild Hunts are often mistaken as a hunt by the Wild Fey but this is not the case, it is a hunt of the wilds where the High Fey and the Fey of their domain hunt the wilds to kill wilds Fey for sport or if they prove strong or useful they get a choice of joining the domain or death. When the World became accessible again the hunts grounds were expanded.

## The Wild Paths
The Wild paths appear as dirt paths leading through the Wilds. These Paths may lead to a location in the World or in an Otherworld domain. There are locations in the Wilds that aren't accessible by Paths that are difficult to find as the Paths tends to shift over time. 
The Wild Paths are the safest way to travel the wilds as the most dangerous creatures and fey of the Wilds live deepest in the wilds.
The Paths shift in appearance, length, and shape. People have tried to mark paths and map them but the changes appear to be unpredictable and markers disappear quickly. Markings on trees appear to heal quickly. The only thing that seems to last are small ribbons tied to tree limbs so those that travel the Paths on a regular basis tends to have multiple colored ribbons ro scraps of fabric tied to themselves, so they can easily add markers as they travel the Paths.
With the dangers of the Wilds and the Wilds Paths many people don't seek them out, however there are those that sneak into the domains of the High Fey on Jobs for various other Fey, Feylings, or humans, some even try to steal or recover items from the Wilds or the domains. There is also a thriving market in the World for Fey Items that might help the humans defend themselves or help their settlements. A common item highly sort after are Fey Stones, stones that absorb the magic of the Otherworld and release it later. Humans have learnt how to use these stones as batteries to power some low powered technology but doing so binds it to the device so it can't be used for any other device. Fey stones allow the World a small remnant of their low powered tech to function if they can be adapted to work.
