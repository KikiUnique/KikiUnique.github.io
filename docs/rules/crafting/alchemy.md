Creation of [Alchemy Items](/rules/crafting/alchemy) such as poisons, acids, and other chemical substances

### Poison
There are a few known common poisons that have known antidotes. When making poisons you need to pick qualities to add to the poison. Each quality has a level, when making the poison you can pick a total level up to your Alchemy level. Once made you need a capsule that details both the roleplay and mechanical effects.

### Acid
Caustic Touch - blade oil that causes bleed, does not affect metal and has not effect unless attack reduces health (no armour or thru call is made)
Eroding Touch - blade oil that causes sharp edges to glide through metal and flesh with ease, allow you to call rend.

### Chemicals
Liquid Armour - a liquid which is a mixture of metal, organic material, and a solvent. When applied to a material with a gap or hole the liquid sticks to the edges leaving behind a material very similar to the original allowing the rest to run off. This allows for quick repairs on armour without needing to remove the armour.

Hollow Maker - a toxic chemical that causes bleed that naturally stops after 5 minutes. During this time something inside you becomes magically inert. You are unable to use magic but also can't be the target of a ritual. If taken within the first minute after a ritual is completed a rituals effects can be removed as if it was not cast.
