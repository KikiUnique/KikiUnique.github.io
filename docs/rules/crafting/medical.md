### Antidotes
There are a few known common poisons that have known antidotes. When making antidotes you need to pick qualities to add to the antidote. Each quality has a level, when making the poison you can pick a total level up to your Medical level. Unlike poison that is made in advance the medicine skill will allow you to diagnose the poison and then attempt to make an antidote. Each antidote quality can remove a poison quality, if the poison doesn't have the quality the picked antidote quality may have adverse effects. When giving antidotes you must wait 1 minute between doses else that cancel each other out.
