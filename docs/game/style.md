{{site.gameName}} is a LARP that encourages PVE play to survive and faction based PVP to maintain control for yourself and your faction.

The setting is designed to take place is an under-populated world that was once similar to ours but has since been over run with creatures more powerful than us. We intend to make it easy for those new to the hobby to get involved without the need for a large investment as basic kit can easily be made using old and lightly modified modern day clothing. For those that enjoy more involved costume, prosthetics, and make-up there are optional character options from character creation and as you progress.

The LARP uses a non-contact combat system but still requires all melee weapons to be LARP safe, ranged weapons must not be loaded or unable to fire projectiles. Nerf guns will be acceptable for firearms.
